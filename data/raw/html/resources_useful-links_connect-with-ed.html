Most Recent Spotlights

Latest spotlights from ED Data Express.
11/19/2024

OESE is happy to announce several new data releases. We now provide Title I school status data, foster care data, and zip files that include chronic absenteeism data with student counts and percentages. In addition, we are now posting the REAP Master Eligibility Spreadsheet.  Check back often to see additional data releases.

Please send questions or comments to EdDataExpress@ed.gov. 

04/12/2024

We've recently updated some of our files and functionality. For SY2122 national grad rate, click here. When using the download customizable download tool, please choose all your filters before selecting states. If you want all states including national rates, click 'Deselect all'  on the states filter.

Send questions or comments about the site to EdDataExpress@ed.gov. 

12/13/2023

Please check out our recently published Title I Part A dashboard. Many of the Title I Part A files are also now available in the customizable download tool.

Send questions or comments about the site to EdDataExpress@ed.gov.

09/15/2023

Check out our SY2122 Chronic Absenteeism, ready in our customizable download tool and data library. 

Earlier this summer we released SY2122 dashboards and files for Title III, Title I Part C (Migrant Education Program) and McKinney Vento (Homeless Children and Youth) and files for REAP. 

Send questions or comments about the site to EdDataExpress@ed.gov. 

09/01/2023

Our latest SY2122 release includes data from Title III and REAP programs.  Click these links to go directly to the SY2122 Title III dashboard, Title III customizable download files, Title III individual files from data library, and these links for REAP customizable download files and REAP files in the data library.  

Earlier in the summer we released SY2122 dashboards and files for Title I Part C (Migrant Education Program) and McKinney Vento (Homeless Children and Youth).   

Send questions or comments about the site to EdDataExpress@ed.gov.
