EDE is designed to support your use of elementary and secondary education data published on this site. With you in mind, the Office of Elementary and Secondary Education (OESE) has built (and continue to build) a set of resources to support your exploration with data. OESE is always looking for ways to improve the site, and welcomes your feedback through email at eddataexpress@ed.gov or our survey found at the top of this page.
